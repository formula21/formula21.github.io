About Me

I love coding!

Hi! My name is Anweshan Roy Chowdhury. I am a student that enjoys studying computer science as my primary subject. I am now pursuing Master of Science in Computer Science at the University of Calcutta. I received an A letter grade and a Master of Science in Computer Science (Honors) Degree from the same university in 2021. I completed my schooling at La Martiniere for Boys, earning the best scores in Computer Science for both my I.S.C. and I.C.S.E.

You may have guessed that I enjoy coding. My major areas of interest include artificial intelligence, backend development, UI/UX development. I also enjoy bridging the gap between old and modern programming languages.

I've learned a number of programming languages throughout the years, some as part of my academic pursuits and others simply out of interest. Python is one of my favourite programming languages. The most current programming languages I learnt are the popular JS Frameworks such as Node.js, React.js, and Express, to mention a few. I've also started the switch from RESTful API to GraphQL API and rapidly adapting to changes wherever possible. However, this does not indicate that I am unfamiliar with PHP, MySQL, or Java. I feel that when these languages are combined with modern languages, they provide some of the greatest outcomes. Recently, I've started merging PHP and Laravel projects with Node.js libraries. One example is using 'socket.io' to track changes to a file and provide users with real-time notifications and UI modifications.

Aside from programming, I enjoy playing my synthesizer, listening to music, binge-watching fascinating shows, and eating a lot of excellent (or perhaps amazing) food (very often, without a reason).

 Basic Hardware

Buy any ordinary laptop with atleast 8 GB of RAM, and if possible stick in another 8 GB RAM.

When my parents learned that I had submitted an application for Computer Science, they wanted me to get a laptop. I was persuaded to purchase a MacBook Air (in 2018), but my aspirations of having any Apple product were dashed due to a silly "purchase restriction."

I had no choice but to use a Windows laptop. However, I want something futuristic (out of the box). Because my Android phone lacked a fingerprint sensor, I ultimately decided on a laptop with Windows Hello support. I eventually purchased a Dell laptop with high-end hardware, and I have no complaints about it to this day.. The specs:

  • (8 x 2) GB DDR4-2666 MHz RAM
  • 2.5 GHz Intel Core i5-7200 7th Gen processor
  • 1TB 5400rpm Serial ATA hard drive
  • Added: 1 TB Samsung 860 EVO SSD (Borrowed from laptop)
  • Graphics Card: AMD Radeon R5 M315 2GB Graphics (My friend gave me his graphics card as he was moving out, so I do not know what is installed at this moment).
