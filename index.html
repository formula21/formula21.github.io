I love coding!

Hey everyone! I'm Anweshan Roy Chowdhury. I'm a student of the University of Calcutta, studying Computer Science. I have recently graduated with a B.Sc (or B.S.) Honours Degree from the University, and am pursuing M.Sc (Master of Science)I finished schooling from La Martinere for Boys, gave my ICSE and ISC examinations, enrolled myself with my favourite subject Computer Science.
